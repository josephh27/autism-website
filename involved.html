NeuroPals by Autism Sense
NeuroPals is a friendly and inclusive Discord server where neurotypical individuals and those on the autism spectrum can come together to learn from and understand each other. This is a safe space where we encourage open dialogue, mutual respect, and the sharing of experiences and knowledge related to autism.

Join us in our mission to promote acceptance, empathy, and understanding for individuals on the autism spectrum!
